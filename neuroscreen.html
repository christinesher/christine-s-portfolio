NeuroScreen

AI-Powered Early Neurodevelopmental Screening

In Progress

About This Project

NeuroScreen is an ongoing product concept exploring how computer vision and physiological data can support early risk screening for autism and ADHD in infants and toddlers.

The project is currently focused on refining the core user flow, validating assumptions with research, and translating clinical constraints into an accessible mobile experience for caregivers.
