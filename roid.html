AI-Powered Fitness & Social App

Project Overview

ROID is a comprehensive fitness and social platform that combines AI-powered personalized recommendations with community engagement. As Product & UI/UX Designer, I designed and developed key interfaces using Figma and Swift in Xcode, focusing on creating an aesthetic and user-friendly experience.

The Problems

  • The old design of Explore page is hard to navigate and lacks aesthetic
  • The Reels page's interface is not correctly aligned
  • Contents on Explore page and Reels are the same
  • Need to seperate two sections and improve the designs

My Role & Contributions

As part of the development team, I focused on both design and implementation:

  • Designed intuitive UI/UX for Explore, Reels, Search, and other minor interfaces on Figma
  • Developed interfaces using Swift in Xcode
  • Researched Stripe-based payment flows for premium features
  • Created responsive layouts optimized for iOS devices

Key Features

AI-Driven Recommendations

Personalized workout and diet plans powered by artificial intelligence, tailored to individual fitness goals and preferences

Social Feed & Community

Connect with other fitness enthusiasts, share progress, and stay motivated through community engagement

Explore Interface

Discover new workouts, nutrition tips, and fitness content curated for your interests

Shop Integration

Seamless shopping experience with Stripe payment integration for premium features and fitness products

Health Data Visualization

Track your progress with intuitive charts and metrics that make it easy to see your fitness journey

Responsive Design

Optimized layouts that work beautifully across all iOS devices
