About Me

Born in Taiwan and moved to the U.S. during middle school, I've always been drawn to anything creative — from sketching to digital art. That curiosity slowly grew into a love for design and how art can shape the way people experience technology. I'm passionate about creating products that feel enjoyable, thoughtful, and visually pleasing to use.

Outside of design, I'm a proud cat mom to a golden lilac British Shorthair. I love spending my free time hanging out with her, usually doing something completely random — and, of course, asking for food halfway through.
