About Me

Born in Taiwan and later moving to the U.S. in middle school, I've always loved anything creative — from sketching to digital art. As I got older, that interest shifted into understanding how people think and why they interact with technology the way they do. Now, I enjoy combining design with user research to create experiences that feel clear, thoughtful, and genuinely helpful.

Outside of design, I love to draw, ski, and spend time with Brûlée! (she's a British Shorthair)
